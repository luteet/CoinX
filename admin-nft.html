Basic Bats

Floor Price 0.08 ETH
Basic Bats is a character collection of 6000 ERC-721 NFTs paired with a play-to-earn NFT based mobile game. The Basic Bats mobile game is nearing completion and will soon be available and free to play on iOS and Android devices. These NFTs have not used generative AI and are completely drawn and curated by hand. The team behind this project is not anonymous and is fully transparent with its community regarding the present and future of Basic Bats. We believe that you shouldn’t need a million followers to make money playing video games and that this project can be a first step to on-boarding the general public into the play to earn potential in the crypto/NFT space. Owning a Basic Bat NFT will allow you to play the mobile game with YOUR character, 3D rendered and playable, as well as giving you access to the competitive community leaderboard. We will be rewarding the top 5 players on the Basic Bats community leaderboard EVERY WEEK with a split of $1000 in ETH.
